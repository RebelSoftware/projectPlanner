**Priority levels:** `P0` (critical) · `P1` (high) · `P2` (medium) · `P3` (low)

**Effort levels:** `S` (small — hours) · `M` (medium — days) · `L` (large — weeks) · `XL` (extra large — months) · `?` (unknown)

**Guidelines:**
- Move tasks between columns as their status changes.
- When a task is blocked, note the blocker and which task/flag it depends on.
- Link tasks to the relevant section in `state/overview.md` for context.
- Populate incrementally — do not save it all for the end.
