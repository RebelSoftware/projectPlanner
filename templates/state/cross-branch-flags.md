**Severity levels:**
- **Info** — Something to be aware of; no action required yet
- **Warning** — May require coordination or adjustment
- **Blocker** — Cannot proceed in affected branch until resolved

**Status values:** `Open` · `Acknowledged` · `Resolved`

**Guidelines:**
- Log flags as they arise — do not batch them.
- Surface warnings and blockers to the developer immediately.
- Info-level flags can be mentioned briefly and left for later review.
- When a flag is resolved, update the status but keep the entry for the audit trail.
